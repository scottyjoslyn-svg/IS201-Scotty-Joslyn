About Me

My journey and experiences

Who I Am

I am currently a Pre-Business student at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. I come from a family of six and am the oldest of four kids, which has played a major role in shaping my sense of responsibility, leadership, and work ethic.

I was born in Oceanside, California, but spent much of my childhood living abroad. I lived in Hong Kong for about ten years, an experience that gave me a global perspective at a young age and helped me become more adaptable and open-minded. Being exposed to different cultures and environments has had a lasting impact on how I view the world.

After returning to the United States, my family settled in Gilbert, Arizona, which I now consider home. That transition helped me develop a stronger sense of independence and identity as I adjusted between very different environments and experiences.

Now at BYU, I am focused on continuing to grow academically and personally while preparing for a career in business. I value hard work, strong relationships, and opportunities that allow me to learn and improve. I am especially interested in leadership, communication, and building a future where I can make a meaningful impact.
