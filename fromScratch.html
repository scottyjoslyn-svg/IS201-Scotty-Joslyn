Mike Trout is one of the greatest baseball players of all time. This page explains why I believe he is the GOAT.

Why I Think Trout Is the GOAT

Mike Trout combines elite hitting, power, speed, and defense at a level very few players in MLB history have achieved. His consistency over time, combined with his peak performance, makes him one of the most complete players ever.
